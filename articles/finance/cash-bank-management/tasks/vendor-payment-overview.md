# <a name="vendor-payment-overview"></a><span data-ttu-id="38fa1-103">Oversikt over leverandørbetaling</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-103">Vendor payment overview</span></span>

[!include [banner](../../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="38fa1-104">Denne oppgaveveiledningen leder deg gjennom forskjellige metoder som brukes til å opprette leverandørbetalinger, inkludert hvordan du bruker et betalingsforslag eller angir en engangsbetaling manuelt.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-104">This task guide will walk you through various methods used to create vendor payments, including how to use a payment proposal or manually entering a one-off payment.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-105">Denne fremgangsmåten bruker demonstrasjonsfirmaet USMF.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-105">This procedure uses the USMF demo company.</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-106">Gå til **Navigasjonsrute > Moduler > Leverandører > Betalinger > Betalingsjournal**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-106">Go to **Navigation pane > Modules > Accounts payable > Payments > Payment journal**.</span></span>
2. <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-107">Klikk på **Ny**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-107">Click **New**.</span></span>
3. <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-108">Velg betalingsjournalen som leverandørbetalingene skal lagres i.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-108">Select the payment journal in which to save the vendor payments.</span></span> 
4. <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-109">Velg journalen, eller angi den manuelt.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-109">Select the journal or manually enter it.</span></span>
5. <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-110">Klikk **Linjer**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-110">Click **Lines**.</span></span>
6. <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-111">Klikk på **Betalingsforslag** i **handlingsruten**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-111">On **Ation pane**, click **Payment proposal**.</span></span>
7. <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-112">Klikk på **Opprett betalingsforslag**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-112">Click **Create payment proposal**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-113">Betalingsforslaget er en spørring som brukes til å velge fakturaer for betaling.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-113">The payment proposal is a query used to select invoices for payment.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-114">Du kan redigere listen over fakturaer som skal betales før du oppretter eller genererer leverandørbetalingene.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-114">You can edit the list of invoices to pay before creating or generating the vendor payments.</span></span>
8. <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-115">Velg fakturaer for betaling etter forfallsdato, kontantrabatt, eller begge deler.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-115">Select invoices for payment by due date, cash discount, or both.</span></span> 
9. <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-116">Angi datoen for sammenligning med forfalt dato eller kontantrabatten.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-116">Enter the date for comparing to the due date or cash discount.</span></span> 
10. <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-117">Valgfritt: Angi en betalingsdato som brukes for alle betalinger.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-117">Optional: Enter a payment date used for all payments.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-118">Datoen som er angitt her, blir den betalingsdatoen for alle fakturaer som er opprettet, uavhengig av forfallsdatoen eller kontantrabattdatoen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-118">The date entered here will be the payment date for all payments created, regardless of the due date or cash discount date.</span></span>  
11. <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-119">Valgfritt: Angi en minste betalingsdato som kan brukes som betalingsdato.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-119">Optional: Enter a minimum payment date which may be used as the payment date.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-120">Den minste betalingsdatoen vil være den tidligste datoen som brukes ved opprettelse av betalinger.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-120">The minimum payment date will be the earliest date used when creating payments.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-121">Hvis for eksempel en faktura har en forfallsdato etter den minste betalingsdatoen, blir forfallsdato betalingsdatoen i stedet for den minste betalingsdatoen for å betale fakturaen på den seneste mulige datoen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-121">For example, if an invoice has a due date after the minimum payment date, the due date will become the payment date instead of the minimum payment date in order to pay the invoice on the latest possible date.</span></span>
12. <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-122">Angi flere spørringsbegrensninger under delen **Poster som skal inkluderes**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-122">Enter additional query restrictions under **Records to include** section.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-123">Filteret brukes ofte til å begrense fakturaene som velges for betaling etter leverandørgruppe eller betalingsmåte.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-123">The filter is often used to restrict the invoices selected for payment by vendor group or method of payment.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-124">Du kan for eksempel legge til et filter for å betale bare fakturaer med sjekk i denne lønnskjøringen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-124">For example, you may add a filter to only pay invoices by check in this pay run.</span></span>
13. <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-125">Angi flere standarder for spørringsbegrensning eller betaling.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-125">Enter additional query restriction or payment defaults.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-126">De ekstra parameterne kan brukes til å definere betalingsvalutaen, eller for å aktivere sentralisert betaling for denne lønnskjøringen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-126">The additional parameters can be used to define the payment currency or to enable centralized payments for this pay run.</span></span>  
14. <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-127">Klikk **OK**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-127">Click **OK**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-128">Når du klikker på **OK**, vises resultatene av spørringen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-128">After clicking **OK**, the results of the query will appear.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-129">Hvis du ikke vil forhåndsvise listen over fakturaene som er valgt for betaling, kan du gå tilbake til hurtigkategorien **Parametere** og endre innstillingen **Opprett betalinger uten forhåndsvisning av faktura** til Ja.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-129">If you don't want to preview the list of invoices selected to pay, you can go back to the **Parameters** fast tab and change the setting **Create payments without invoice preview** to "Yes".</span></span>  
15. <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-130">Velg knappen **Vis betalingsoversikt** for å vise betalingene som skal opprettes for leverandøren på fakturaen som er valgt.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-130">Choose the **Show payment overview** button to view the payments that will be created for the vendor on the invoice selected.</span></span>
16. <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-131">Velg **Skjul betalingsoversikt** for å skjule betalingene.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-131">Choose the **Hide payment overview** button to hide the payments.</span></span> 
17. <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-132">Klikk **Opprett betalinger**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-132">Click **Create payments**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-133">Før du velger **Opprett betalinger**, kan du høyreklikke i rutenettet og eksportere en liste over fakturaene til Excel.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-133">Before choosing **Create payments**, you can right click on the grid and export the list of invoices to Excel.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-134">Knappen **Opprett betalinger** oppretter leverandørbetalingene i betalingsjournalen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-134">The **Create payments** button will create the vendor payments in the payment journal.</span></span>  
18. <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-135">Skann betalingene og kontroller at betalingsmåten er definert for alle betalinger.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-135">Scan your payments and make sure the method of payment is defined for all payments.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-136">Hvis du genererer betalingene, for eksempel skrive ut en sjekk eller opprette en elektronisk betaling, må betalingsmåten defineres.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-136">If you generate the payments, such as printing a check or creating an electronic payment, the method of payment must be defined.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-137">Betalingsmåten vil også angi standard bankkonto som betalingen skal skje fra.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-137">The method of payment will also default the bank account from the payment will be made.</span></span>  
19. <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-138">Klikk på **Ny** for å opprette en engangsbetaling.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-138">Click **New** to create a one-off payment.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-139">En engangsbetaling kan legges til i en betalingsjournal når som helst før postering.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-139">A one-off payment can be added to a payment journal at any time prior to posting.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-140">Dette gjøres ved å klikke på **Ny** og legge til betalingsinformasjonen manuelt, i stedet for å bruke **Betalingsforslag**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-140">This is done by clicking the **New** button and adding the payment information manually, rather than using the **Payment proposal**.</span></span>  
20. <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-141">Velg leverandøren som betalingen skal utføres til.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-141">Select the vendor to whom the payment will be made.</span></span>
21. <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-142">Hvis det finnes en faktura å betale, velger du **Utlign transaksjoner** for å velge fakturaen for betaling.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-142">If an invoice exists to pay, select **Settle transactions** to select the invoice for payment.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-143">Hvis dette er en forskuddsbetaling, er dette trinnet valgfritt.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-143">If this is a prepayment, this step is optional.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-144">Du kan opprette betalingen uten å merke noen fakturaer.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-144">You can create the payment without selecting any invoice.</span></span> 
22. <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-145">Merk alle fakturaer som skal betales.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-145">Mark any invoices that will be paid.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-146">Hvis du bruker **Utlign transaksjoner** til å velge fakturaer for betaling, beregnes betalingsbeløpet automatisk basert på hvilke fakturaer du merker for betaling og hvilket beløp du angir i **Beløp som skal utlignes**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-146">If you use the **Settle transactions** to select the invoices for payment, the payment amount will automatically be calculated based on what invoices you mark for payment, and what amount you enter in the **Amount to settle**.</span></span>
23. <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-147">Klikk **OK**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-147">Click **OK**.</span></span>
24. <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-148">Hvis du vil slette en betaling, merker du raden.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-148">If you want to delete a payment, mark the row.</span></span>
25. <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-149">Klikk **Slett**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-149">Click **Delete**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-150">Hvis du sletter en betaling, slettes bare betalingen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-150">Deleting a payment will only delete the payment.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-151">Alle fakturaer som er merket for betaling vil fremdeles være tilgjengelig for betaling med en annen betaling.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-151">Any invoices marked for payment will still be available to be paid by another payment.</span></span>
26. <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-152">Klikk **Ja**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-152">Click **Yes**.</span></span>
27. <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-153">Velg **Generer betaling** for å skrive ut sjekker eller opprette den elektroniske betalingsfilen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-153">Choose **Generate payment** to print checks or create the electronic payment file.</span></span>
28. <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-154">Velg betalingsmåten som du vil generere.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-154">Select the method of payment that you want to generate.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-155">Betalingsjournalen kan inneholde betalinger for både sjekker og elektroniske betalinger, men du kan bare generere en betalingstype om gangen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-155">The payment journal can contain payments for both Checks and electronic payments, but you can only generate one payment type at a time.</span></span>
29. <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-156">Velg bankkontoen som du vil generere betalingene fra.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-156">Select the bank account from which to generate the payments.</span></span>
30. <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-157">Klikk **OK**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-157">Click **OK**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-158">Betalinger genereres bare for betalinger som samsvarer med valgt **Betalingsmåte** og **Bankkonto**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-158">Payments will only be generated for payments that match the **Method of payment** and **Bank account** you selected.</span></span>
31. <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-159">Hvis du genererer **Sjekker**, velger du **Dokument** for å sikre riktig utskriftsmål for sjekkene.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-159">If you are generating **Checks**, choose **Document** to ensure the correct print destination for the checks.</span></span>
32. <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-160">Klikk **OK**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-160">Click **OK**.</span></span>
33. <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-161">Klikk på **OK** for å generere betalingene.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-161">Click **OK** to generate the payments.</span></span>
34. <span data-ttu-id="38fa1-162">Klikk på **Poster** hvis alle betalingene er godkjent og generert.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38fa1-162">Click **Post** if all the payments are approved and generated.</span></span>
