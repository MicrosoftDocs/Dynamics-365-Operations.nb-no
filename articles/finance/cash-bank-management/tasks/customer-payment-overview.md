1. <span data-ttu-id="2619f-106">Gå til **Moduler > Kunder > Betalinger > Betalingsjournal** i navigasjonsruten.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2619f-106">Go to **Navigation pane > Modules > Accounts receivable > Payments > Payment journal**.</span></span>
2. <span data-ttu-id="2619f-107">Klikk på **Ny**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2619f-107">Click **New**.</span></span>
3. <span data-ttu-id="2619f-108">Velg betalingsjournalen som kundebetalingene skal lagres i.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2619f-108">Select the payment journal which the customer payments will be saved.</span></span>
4. <span data-ttu-id="2619f-109">Velg eller angi journalen manuelt.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2619f-109">Select or manually enter the journal.</span></span>
5. <span data-ttu-id="2619f-110">Klikk på **Angi kundebetalinger** **handlingsruten**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2619f-110">On **Action pane**, click **Enter customer payments**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2619f-111">Angi kundebetalinger som brukes til å registrere én kundebetaling om gangen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2619f-111">Enter customer payments is used to record one customer payment at a time.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2619f-112">Du kan angi betalingsinformasjon øverst, og deretter kan du merke fakturaene som ble betalt med betalingen, alt fra samme side.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2619f-112">You enter the payment information at the top, and then you can mark the invoices that were paid by the payment, all from the same page.</span></span>  
6. <span data-ttu-id="2619f-113">Angi kunden som du har mottatt betalingen fra.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2619f-113">Enter the customer from whom you received the payment.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2619f-114">Hvis du ikke kjenner til kunden, men kjenner en transaksjon som ble betalt, kan du bruke feltet Transaksjon til å angi betalingen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2619f-114">If you don't know the customer but do know a transaction that was paid, you can use the Transaction field to enter the payment.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2619f-115">Angi eller velg fakturaen i feltet Transaksjon.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2619f-115">Enter or select the invoice in the Transaction field.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2619f-116">Kunden angis som standard automatisk når du har valgt transaksjonen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2619f-116">The customer will automatically default after you select the transaction.</span></span>
7. <span data-ttu-id="2619f-117">I feltet **Betalingsreferanse** angir du en betalingsreferanse.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2619f-117">In the **Payment reference** field, enter a payment reference.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2619f-118">Betalingsreferansen kan være kundens sjekknummer eller en referanse fra kundens elektroniske betaling.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2619f-118">The payment reference could be the customer's check number or a reference from the customer's electronic payment.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2619f-119">Betalingsreferansen er bare nødvendig hvis du merker å inkludere betalingen på et betalingsbilag.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2619f-119">The payment reference is only required if you mark to include the payment on a deposit slip.</span></span>  
8. <span data-ttu-id="2619f-120">I feltet **Betalingsbilag** velger du om betalingen skal tas med i et betalingsbilag.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2619f-120">In the **Deposit slip** field, select whether the payment will be included on a deposit slip.</span></span> 
9. <span data-ttu-id="2619f-121">Angi beløpet for kundebetalingen i **Beløp**-feltet.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2619f-121">In the **Amount** field, enter the amount of the customer payment.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2619f-122">Betalingsbeløpet angis ikke som standard.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2619f-122">The payment amount will not default.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2619f-123">Det må angis manuelt.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2619f-123">It must be manually entered.</span></span> 
10. <span data-ttu-id="2619f-124">Merk fakturaene som ble betalt av kunden.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2619f-124">Mark the invoices that were paid by the customer.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2619f-125">Hvis betalingen er en forskuddsbetaling, trenger du ikke å merke alle fakturaer for utligning.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2619f-125">If the payment is a prepayment, you are not required to mark any invoices for settlement.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2619f-126">Betalingen kan fremdeles lagres og posteres.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2619f-126">The payment can still be saved and posted.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2619f-127">Utligning mot en faktura kan skje på et senere tidspunkt.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2619f-127">Settlement to an invoice can happen at a later time.</span></span>
11. <span data-ttu-id="2619f-128">Angi betalingsbeløpet som skal utlignes mot den merkede fakturaen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2619f-128">Enter the amount of the payment that should be settled to the marked invoice.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2619f-129">Dette feltet kan brukes når betalingen gjelder en delbetaling.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2619f-129">This field can be used when the payment is for a partial payment.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2619f-130">Hvis du ikke angir et beløp, bestemmes beløpet som skal utlignes automatisk for deg.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2619f-130">If you don't enter an amount, the amount to settle will automatically be determined for you.</span></span>
12. <span data-ttu-id="2619f-131">Klikk på **Lagre i journal**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2619f-131">Click **Save in journal**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2619f-132">Før du lagrer betalingen i journalen, må du kontrollere at motkontoen er definert.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2619f-132">Before you save the payment to the journal, make sure that the offset account is defined.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2619f-133">Ved hjelp av **Lagre i journal** lagres betalingen og flyttes til journalen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2619f-133">Using **Save in journal** will save the payment and move it to the journal.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2619f-134">Du kan deretter fortsette å angi den neste betalingen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2619f-134">You can then continue entering the next payment.</span></span>
13. <span data-ttu-id="2619f-135">Lukk siden.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2619f-135">Close the page.</span></span>
14. <span data-ttu-id="2619f-136">I **Handlingsrute** klikker du på Linjer.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2619f-136">On **Action pane**, click Lines.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2619f-137">Når du åpner Linjer, vises eventuelle betalinger du registrert på siden **Angi kundebetalinger** og lagret i journalen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2619f-137">When opening Lines, you will see any payments you recorded on the **Enter customer payments** page and saved into the journal.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2619f-138">Du kan også bruke denne siden til å angi nye kundebetalinger, eller redigere eksisterende kundebetalinger før de posteres.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2619f-138">You can also use this page to enter new customer payments, or edit existing customer payment before they are posted.</span></span>
15. <span data-ttu-id="2619f-139">Klikk på **Ny** for å opprette en annen betaling.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2619f-139">Click **New** to create another payment.</span></span> 
16. <span data-ttu-id="2619f-140">Velg kunden som du har mottatt betalingen fra.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2619f-140">Select the customer from whom you received the payment.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2619f-141">Hvis du ikke kjenner til kunden, men kjenner en faktura betalt av betalingen, kan du bruke feltet Faktura til å manuelt angi eller velge fakturaen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2619f-141">If you don't know the customer but know an invoice paid by the payment, use the Invoice field to manually enter or select the invoice.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2619f-142">Kunden angis som standard når fakturaen er valgt.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2619f-142">The customer will default after the invoice is selected.</span></span>  
17. <span data-ttu-id="2619f-143">Klikk **Utlign transaksjoner** hvis du vil merke fakturaene som ble betalt.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2619f-143">Click **Settle transactions** to mark invoices that were paid.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2619f-144">Du trenger ikke å utligne betalingen mot noen fakturaer.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2619f-144">You are not required to settle the payment to any invoices.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2619f-145">Hvis dette er en forskuddsbetaling, eller hvis du ikke vet hvilken faktura som er betalt, kan du angi og postere betalingen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2619f-145">If this is a prepayment or if you don't know what invoice was paid, you can enter and post the payment.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2619f-146">Betalingen kan utlignes mot en faktura på et senere tidspunkt.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2619f-146">The payment can be settled to an invoice at a later point.</span></span>  
18. <span data-ttu-id="2619f-147">Merk fakturaene som betalt av betalingen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2619f-147">Mark the invoices paid by the payment.</span></span> 
19. <span data-ttu-id="2619f-148">I **Beløp**-feltet angir du betalingsbeløpet som skal utlignes mot fakturaen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2619f-148">In the **Amount** field, enter the amount of the payment that will be settled to the invoice.</span></span>
20. <span data-ttu-id="2619f-149">Klikk **OK**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2619f-149">Click **OK**.</span></span>
21. <span data-ttu-id="2619f-150">I feltet **Betalingsreferanse** angir du en betalingsreferanse.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2619f-150">In the **Payment reference** field, enter a payment reference.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2619f-151">Betalingsreferansen er bare nødvendig hvis du merker å inkludere betalingen på et betalingsbilag.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2619f-151">The payment reference is only required if you mark to include the payment on a deposit slip.</span></span>  
22. <span data-ttu-id="2619f-152">Klikk på **Poster** i **handlingsruten** for å postere kundebetalingene.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2619f-152">On **Action pane**, click **Post** to post the customer payments.</span></span>
