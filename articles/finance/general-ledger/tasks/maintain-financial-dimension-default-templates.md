9. <span data-ttu-id="41ee2-115">Velg **Legg til**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="41ee2-115">Select **Add**.</span></span>
10. <span data-ttu-id="41ee2-116">Angi en ny prosent, som gjør at den totale prosenten blir 100 %.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="41ee2-116">Enter another percent, which will make the total percent equal to 100%.</span></span>
