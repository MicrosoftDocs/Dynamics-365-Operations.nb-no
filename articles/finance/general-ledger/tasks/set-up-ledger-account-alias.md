# <a name="set-up-a-ledger-account-alias"></a><span data-ttu-id="ed02c-103">Definere et finanskontoalias</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ed02c-103">Set up a ledger account alias</span></span>

[!include [banner](../../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="ed02c-104">Denne fremgangsmåten viser hvordan du oppretter et kontoalias som inneholder en snarvei for å angi et kontonummer.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ed02c-104">This procedure shows how to create an account alias that provides a shortcut for entering an account number.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ed02c-105">Denne prosedyren bruker demonstrasjonsdatafirmaet USMF.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ed02c-105">This procedure uses demo data company USMF.</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="ed02c-106">Gå til Økonomimodul > Kontoplan > Kontoer > Alias for finanskonto.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ed02c-106">Go to General ledger > Chart of accounts > Accounts > Ledger account alias.</span></span>
2. <span data-ttu-id="ed02c-107">Klikk Ny.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ed02c-107">Click New.</span></span>
3. <span data-ttu-id="ed02c-108">Skriv inn en verdi i feltet Alias for finanskonto.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ed02c-108">In the Ledger account alias field, type a value.</span></span>
4. <span data-ttu-id="ed02c-109">Velg strukturen som kontoen og dimensjonene tilhører, i feltet Kontostruktur.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ed02c-109">In the Account structure field, select the structure the account and dimensions belong to.</span></span>
5. <span data-ttu-id="ed02c-110">Klikk rullegardinknappen i feltet Firma for å åpne oppslaget.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ed02c-110">In the Company field, click the drop-down button to open the lookup.</span></span>
6. <span data-ttu-id="ed02c-111">Finn og velg firmaet som aliaset gjelder for, i listen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ed02c-111">In the list, find and select the company that the alias applies to.</span></span>
7. <span data-ttu-id="ed02c-112">Klikk koblingen i den valgte raden i listen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ed02c-112">In the list, click the link in the selected row.</span></span>
8. <span data-ttu-id="ed02c-113">I feltet Definisjon av finanskontoalias angir du kontoen og dimensjonene.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ed02c-113">In the Ledger account alias definition field, specify the account and dimensions.</span></span>
    * <span data-ttu-id="ed02c-114">Kontoen og dimensjonene fylles ut når du bruker snarveien.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ed02c-114">The account and dimensions will be populated when using the shortcut.</span></span>  
9. <span data-ttu-id="ed02c-115">Velg dimensjonen som skal ha fokus når aliaset brukes, i feltet Innledende fokus.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ed02c-115">In the Initial focus field, select the dimension that will have focus when the alias is used.</span></span>
    * <span data-ttu-id="ed02c-116">Når du har skrevet inn snarveien, og kontoen og dimensjonene er utfylt, flyttes markøren eller fokus til feltet Innledende fokus.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ed02c-116">After you type the shortcut, and the account and dimensions are populated, the Initial focus field is where the cursor or focus will move to.</span></span>
