# <a name="create-a-legal-entity"></a><span data-ttu-id="7ff39-103">Opprette en ny juridisk enhet</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7ff39-103">Create a legal entity</span></span>

[!include [banner](../../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="7ff39-104">En juridisk enhet er en organisasjon som identifiseres via registrering hos en juridisk myndighet.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7ff39-104">A legal entity is an organization that is identified through registration with a legal authority.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7ff39-105">Juridiske enheter kan inngå kontrakter og er påkrevd for å klargjøre oppgjør som rapporterer om prestasjonen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7ff39-105">Legal entities can enter into contracts and are required to prepare statements that report on their performance.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7ff39-106">Fremgangsmåten nedenfor forklarer hvordan du oppretter en juridisk enhet.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7ff39-106">The following procedure explains how to create a legal entity.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7ff39-107">Demonstrasjonsdatafirmaet USMF brukes til å opprette denne fremgangsmåten.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7ff39-107">The demo data company used to create this procedure is USMF.</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="7ff39-108">Gå til **Navigasjonsrute > Moduler > Organisasjonsstyring > Organisasjoner > Juridiske enheter**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7ff39-108">Go to **Navigation pane > Modules > Organization administration > Organizations > Legal entities**.</span></span>
2. <span data-ttu-id="7ff39-109">Klikk på **Ny**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7ff39-109">Click **New**.</span></span>
3. <span data-ttu-id="7ff39-110">Skriv inn en verdi i **Navn**-feltet.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7ff39-110">In the **Name** field, type a value.</span></span>
4. <span data-ttu-id="7ff39-111">Skriv inn en verdi i feltet **Firma.**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7ff39-111">In the **Company** field, type a value.</span></span>
5. <span data-ttu-id="7ff39-112">Angi eller velg en verdi i **Land/område**-feltet.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7ff39-112">In the **Country/region** field, enter or select a value.</span></span>
6. <span data-ttu-id="7ff39-113">Klikk på **OK**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7ff39-113">Click **OK**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7ff39-114">I delen **Generelt** angir du følgende generelle informasjon om den juridiske enheten: Angi et søkenavn, hvis det kreves et søkenavn.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7ff39-114">In the **General** section, provide the following general information about the legal entity: Enter a search name, if a search name is required.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7ff39-115">Et søkenavn er et alternativt navn som kan brukes til å søke etter den juridiske enheten.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7ff39-115">A search name is an alternate name that can be used to search for this legal entity.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7ff39-116">Velg om den juridiske enheten brukes som et konsoliderte firma.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7ff39-116">Select whether this legal entity is being used as a consolidation company.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7ff39-117">Velg om den juridiske enheten brukes som et elimineringsfirma.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7ff39-117">Select whether this legal entity is being used as an elimination company.</span></span> 
7. <span data-ttu-id="7ff39-118">Vis delen **Adresser**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7ff39-118">Expand the **Addresses** section.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7ff39-119">I **Adresser**-delen klikker du på **Rediger** for å angi adresseinformasjon, for eksempel gatenavn og -nummer, postnummer og poststed.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7ff39-119">In the **Addresses** section, click **Edit** to enter address information, such as the street name and number, postal code, and city.</span></span>
8. <span data-ttu-id="7ff39-120">Vis delen **Kontaktinformasjon**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7ff39-120">Expand the **Contact information** section.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7ff39-121">I delen **Kontaktinformasjon** angir du informasjon om kommunikasjonsmetoder, for eksempel e-postadresse, URL-adresser og telefonnumre.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7ff39-121">In the **Contact information** section, enter information about methods of communication, such as email addresses, URLs, and telephone numbers.</span></span> 
9. <span data-ttu-id="7ff39-122">Vis delen **Lovbestemt rapportering**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7ff39-122">Expand the **Statutory reporting** section.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7ff39-123">I delen **Lovbestemt rapportering** skriver du inn registreringsnumrene som brukes ved lovbestemt rapportering.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7ff39-123">In the **Statutory reporting** section, enter the registration numbers that are used for statutory reporting.</span></span>
10. <span data-ttu-id="7ff39-124">Vis delen **Registreringsnumre**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7ff39-124">Expand the **Registration numbers** section.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7ff39-125">I delen **Registreringsnumre** angir du eventuell informasjon som kreves av den juridiske enheten.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7ff39-125">In the **Registration numbers** section, enter any information required by the legal entity.</span></span>  
11. <span data-ttu-id="7ff39-126">Vis delen **Bankkontoinformasjon**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7ff39-126">Expand the **Bank account information** section.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7ff39-127">I delen **Bankkontoinformasjon** angir du bankkontoer og registreringsnumre for den juridiske enheten.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7ff39-127">In the **Bank account information** section, enter bank accounts and routing numbers for the legal entity.</span></span>
12. <span data-ttu-id="7ff39-128">Vis delen **Utenrikshandel og logistikk**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7ff39-128">Expand the **Foreign trade and logistics** section.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7ff39-129">I delen **Utenrikshandel og logistikk** angir du forsendelsesinformasjon for den juridiske enheten.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7ff39-129">In the **Foreign trade and logistics** section, enter shipping information for the legal entity.</span></span>  
13. <span data-ttu-id="7ff39-130">Vis delen **Nummerserier**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7ff39-130">Expand the **Number sequences** section.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7ff39-131">I delen **Nummerserier** kan du vise nummerseriene som er tilknyttet den juridiske enheten.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7ff39-131">In the **Number sequences** section, you can view the number sequences that are associated with the legal entity.</span></span>  
14. <span data-ttu-id="7ff39-132">Vis **Bilder**-delen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7ff39-132">Expand the **Images** section.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7ff39-133">I delen **Bilder** viser eller endrer du logoen og/eller instrumentbordbildet som er knyttet til den juridiske enheten.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7ff39-133">In the **Images** section, view or change the logo and/or dashboard image that are associated with the legal entity.</span></span>  
15. <span data-ttu-id="7ff39-134">Vis delen **Avgiftsregistrering**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7ff39-134">Expand the **Tax registration** section.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7ff39-135">I delen **Avgiftsregistrering** skriver du inn registreringsnumrene som brukes til å rapportere til skattemyndighetene.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7ff39-135">In the **Tax registration** section, enter the registration numbers that are used to report to tax authorities.</span></span>
16. <span data-ttu-id="7ff39-136">Vis delen **1099-avgift**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7ff39-136">Expand the **Tax 1099** section.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7ff39-137">I delen **1099-avgift** skriver du inn 1099-informasjon for den juridiske enheten.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7ff39-137">In the **Tax 1099** section, enter 1099 information for the legal entity.</span></span>  
17. <span data-ttu-id="7ff39-138">Klikk på **Lagre**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7ff39-138">Click **Save**.</span></span>
