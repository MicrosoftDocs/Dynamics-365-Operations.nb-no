## <a name="financial-dimension-format-to-msdyn_financialdimensionformats"></a>Finansdimensjonsformat til msdyn_financialdimensionformats

Denne malen synkroniserer data mellom Finance and Operations-apper og Common Data Service.

Finance and Operations-felt | Tilordningstype | Annet Dynamics 365-felt | Standardverdi
---|---|---|---
DIMENSIONFORMATNAME | = | msdyn_dimensionformatname | 
DIMENSIONFORMATTYPE | >< | msdyn_dimensionformattype | 
FINANCIALDIMENSIONFORMAT | = | msdyn_financialdimensionformat | 
ISACTIVE | >< | msdyn_isactive |
