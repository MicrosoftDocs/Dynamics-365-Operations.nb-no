# <a name="set-up-template-to-represent-objects-at-customer-site"></a><span data-ttu-id="8b045-103">Definere mal som skal representere objekter hos kunden</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8b045-103">Set up template to represent objects at customer site</span></span> 

[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]


<span data-ttu-id="8b045-104">Bruk denne fremgangsmåten til å definere en mal som representerer objekter hos en kunde.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8b045-104">Use this procedure to set up a template that represents objects at a customer site.</span></span>

1.  <span data-ttu-id="8b045-105">Klikk på **Servicestyring** \> **Felles** \> **Serviceavtaler** \> **Serviceavtaler**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8b045-105">Click **Service management** \> **Common** \> **Service agreements** \> **Service agreements**.</span></span>

2.  <span data-ttu-id="8b045-106">Dobbeltklikk en serviceavtale, eller klikk **Serviceavtale** for å opprette en ny serviceavtale.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8b045-106">Double-click a service agreement, or click **Service agreement** to create a new service agreement.</span></span>

3.  <span data-ttu-id="8b045-107">Klikk på fanen **Oppsett**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8b045-107">Click the **Setup** tab.</span></span>

4.  <span data-ttu-id="8b045-108">Klikk på **Serviceobjekter** for å legge ved en malstykkliste i serviceavtalen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8b045-108">Click **Service objects** to attach a template bill of materials (BOM) to the service agreement.</span></span>

5.  <span data-ttu-id="8b045-109">I skjemaet **Serviceobjekter** velger du serviceobjektet du vil knytte til en malstykkliste, og klikk deretter **Funksjoner** \> **Tilknytt malstykkliste**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8b045-109">In the **Service objects** form, select a service object, and then click **Functions** \> **Attach template BOM**.</span></span>

7.  <span data-ttu-id="8b045-111">Klikk på **Designer** i skjemaet **Serviceobjekter** for å endre linjene i stykklistemalen slik at stykklisten representerer de objektene du ønsker.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8b045-111">In the **Service objects** form, click **Designer** to modify the template BOM so that the BOM represents the objects that you want.</span></span>

8.  <span data-ttu-id="8b045-112">Lukk skjemaene for å lagre endringene.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8b045-112">Close the forms to save your changes.</span></span>
