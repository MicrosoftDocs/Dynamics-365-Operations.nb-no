[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]


<span data-ttu-id="e9f53-104">Bruk denne fremgangsmåten til å definere en mal som representerer objekter hos en kunde.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e9f53-104">Use this procedure to set up a template that represents objects at a customer site.</span></span>

2.  <span data-ttu-id="e9f53-106">Dobbeltklikk en serviceavtale, eller klikk **Serviceavtale** for å opprette en ny serviceavtale.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e9f53-106">Double-click a service agreement, or click **Service agreement** to create a new service agreement.</span></span>

3.  <span data-ttu-id="e9f53-107">Klikk kategorien **Oppsett**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e9f53-107">Click the **Setup** tab.</span></span>
