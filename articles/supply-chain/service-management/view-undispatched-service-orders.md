# <a name="view-undispatched-service-orders"></a><span data-ttu-id="a43ee-103">Vise ikke fordelte serviceordrer</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a43ee-103">View undispatched service orders</span></span> 

[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]


<span data-ttu-id="a43ee-104">Dette emnet beskriver hvordan du viser aktiviteter i en serviceordre som ikke har en arbeider tilordnet til dem fra en fordelingsgruppe.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a43ee-104">This topic describes how to view activities in a service order that don’t have a worker assigned to them from a dispatch team.</span></span>

1.  <span data-ttu-id="a43ee-105">Klikk **Servicestyring** \> **Periodisk** \> **Tjenestefordeling** .</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a43ee-105">Click **Service management** \> **Periodic** \> **Dispatch board** .</span></span>

2.  <span data-ttu-id="a43ee-106">I **Tjenestefordeling** -vinduet angir du startdatoen og sluttdatoen for perioden du vil vise fordelingsaktivitet for.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a43ee-106">In the **Dispatch board** window, enter the start date and end date for the period to view dispatch activity for.</span></span>

3.  <span data-ttu-id="a43ee-107">Valgfritt: Velg om du vil ta med lukkede aktiviteter og beslektede team i resultatene.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a43ee-107">Optional: Select whether to include closed activities and related teams in the results.</span></span>

4.  <span data-ttu-id="a43ee-108">Klikk **OK** for å vise resultatene.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a43ee-108">Click **OK** to view the results.</span></span>
    

    > [!NOTE]
    > <P><span data-ttu-id="a43ee-109">I <STRONG>Tjenestefordeling</STRONG>-skjemaet vises <STRONG>Avansert</STRONG>-visningen automatisk.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a43ee-109">In the <STRONG>Dispatch board</STRONG> form, the <STRONG>Advanced</STRONG> view is automatically displayed.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="a43ee-110">I denne visningen vises Gantt-diagrammet øverst på siden og kategoriene nederst på siden.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a43ee-110">In this view, the Gantt chart at the top of the page and the tabs at the bottom of the page are displayed.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="a43ee-111">Klikk <STRONG>Enkel</STRONG> for å vise bare Gantt-diagrammet.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a43ee-111">Click <STRONG>Simple</STRONG> to display only the Gantt chart.</span></span></P>
