[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]


1.  <span data-ttu-id="b2158-104">Klikk på **Servicestyring** \> **Felles** \> **Serviceavtaler** \> **Serviceavtaler**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b2158-104">Click **Service management** \> **Common** \> **Service agreements** \> **Service agreements**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="b2158-105">Velg serviceavtalen som du vil slette en servicestykkliste fra.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b2158-105">Select the service agreement that you want to delete a service bill of materials (BOM) from.</span></span>

2.  <span data-ttu-id="b2158-106">Klikk på **Serviceobjekter** i **handlingsruten**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b2158-106">On the **Action Pane**, click **Service objects**.</span></span>

3.  <span data-ttu-id="b2158-107">I **Serviceobjekter**-skjemaet velger du serviceobjektet som du vil slette en servicestykkliste fra.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b2158-107">In the **Service objects** form, select the service object that you want to delete a service BOM from.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="b2158-108">Klikk på **Funksjoner**, og velg deretter **Slett servicestykkliste**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b2158-108">Click **Functions**, and then select **Delete service BOM**.</span></span>

4.  <span data-ttu-id="b2158-109">Klikk på **Ja**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b2158-109">Click **Yes**.</span></span>
