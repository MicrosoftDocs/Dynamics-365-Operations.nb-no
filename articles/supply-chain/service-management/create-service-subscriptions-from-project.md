[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]


<span data-ttu-id="66488-104">Du kan opprette et serviceabonnement fra et prosjekt i **Prosjektstyring og regnskap**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="66488-104">You can create a service subscription from a project in **Project management and accounting**.</span></span>

1.  <span data-ttu-id="66488-105">Klikk på **Prosjektstyring og regnskap** \> **Felles** \> **Prosjekter** \> **Alle prosjekter**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="66488-105">Click **Project management and accounting** \> **Common** \> **Projects** \> **All projects**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="66488-106">Åpne prosjektet som du vil opprette et abonnement for.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="66488-106">Open the project that you want to create a subscription for.</span></span>

2.  <span data-ttu-id="66488-107">I **Prosjekter**-skjemaet i **handlingsruten** på fanen **Administrer** klikker du på **Service** \> **Serviceabonnement**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="66488-107">On the **Projects** form, on the **Action Pane**, on the **Manage** tab, click **Service** \> **Service subscription**.</span></span>

> <P><span data-ttu-id="66488-112">Hvis du har angitt en nummerserie for abonnementsgruppen, genereres det automatisk en abonnements-ID for abonnementet når du angir abonnementsgruppen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="66488-112">If you have set up a number sequence for the subscription group, a subscription ID is generated automatically for the subscription when you specify the subscription group.</span></span></P>
