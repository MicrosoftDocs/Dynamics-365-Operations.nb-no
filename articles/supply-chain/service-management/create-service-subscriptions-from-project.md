[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]


<span data-ttu-id="7d59b-104">Du kan opprette et serviceabonnement fra et prosjekt i **Prosjektstyring og regnskap**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7d59b-104">You can create a service subscription from a project in **Project management and accounting**.</span></span>
