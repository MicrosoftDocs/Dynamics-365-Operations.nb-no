# <a name="attach-templates-to-service-objects"></a><span data-ttu-id="bff7b-103">Tilknytte maler til serviceobjekter</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="bff7b-103">Attach templates to service objects</span></span>    

[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]


<span data-ttu-id="bff7b-104">En stykklistemal kan knyttes til en serviceobjektrelasjon.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="bff7b-104">A template bill of materials (BOM) can be attached to a service object relation.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="bff7b-105">Serviceobjektrelasjonen kan deretter knyttes til enten serviceordrer eller serviceavtaler.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="bff7b-105">The service object relation can then be associated with either service orders or service agreements.</span></span>

1.  <span data-ttu-id="bff7b-106">Klikk på **Servicestyring** \> **Oppsett** \> **Serviceobjekter** \> **Serviceobjekter**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="bff7b-106">Click **Service management** \> **Setup** \> **Service objects** \> **Service objects**.</span></span>

2.  <span data-ttu-id="bff7b-107">Velg et serviceobjekt.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="bff7b-107">Select a service object.</span></span>
