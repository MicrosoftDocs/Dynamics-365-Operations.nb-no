2. <span data-ttu-id="154d7-107">Dobbeltklikk en serviceavtale for å åpne **Serviceavtaler**-siden.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="154d7-107">Double-click a service agreement to open the **Service agreements** page.</span></span>

4. <span data-ttu-id="154d7-109">I kategorien **Trekontroll** utvider du trestrukturen, finner malen som inneholder linjene du vil kopiere til serviceavtalen, og velger deretter servicemalen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="154d7-109">On the **Tree control** tab, expand the tree, find the template that contains the lines that you want to copy to the service agreement, and then select the service template.</span></span>

7. <span data-ttu-id="154d7-112">Lukk sidene for å lagre endringene.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="154d7-112">Close the pages to save your changes.</span></span>
