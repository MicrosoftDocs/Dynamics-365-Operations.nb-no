## <a name="credit-subscription-transactions"></a><span data-ttu-id="76bed-104">Kreditere abonnementstransaksjoner</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="76bed-104">Credit subscription transactions</span></span>

1.  <span data-ttu-id="76bed-105">Klikk på **Servicestyring** \> **Felles** \> **Serviceabonnementer** \> **Alle serviceabonnementer**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="76bed-105">Click **Service management** \> **Common** \> **Service subscriptions** \> **All service subscriptions**.</span></span>

2.  <span data-ttu-id="76bed-106">Velg abonnementet som er knyttet til abonnementstransaksjonen du vil opprette en kreditnota for.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="76bed-106">Select the subscription attached to the subscription transaction for which you want to create a credit note.</span></span>

3.  <span data-ttu-id="76bed-107">Velg kategorien **Analyser** i handlingsruten, og klikk deretter **Gebyrtransaksjoner**-knappen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="76bed-107">Select the **Analyze** tab, and then click the **Fee transactions** button on the Action Pane.</span></span>

4.  <span data-ttu-id="76bed-108">I skjemaet **Gebyrtransaksjoner** velger du transaksjonen som du vil opprette en kreditnota for.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="76bed-108">From the **Fee transactions** form, select the transaction for which you want to create a credit note.</span></span>

5.  <span data-ttu-id="76bed-109">Klikk på **Funksjoner** \> **Velg for kreditnota**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="76bed-109">Click **Functions** \> **Select for credit note**.</span></span>

6.  <span data-ttu-id="76bed-110">Fra skjemaet **Velg for kreditnota** velger du transaksjonen du vil kreditere, og deretter velger du **OK**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="76bed-110">From the **Select for credit note** form, select the transaction that you want to credit and then click **OK**.</span></span>


> [!NOTE]
> <P><span data-ttu-id="76bed-111">Når du oppretter kreditnotaen, må du passe på at du velger <STRONG>Kreditnotaer</STRONG>.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="76bed-111">When you create the credit note, make sure that you select <STRONG>Credit notes</STRONG>.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="76bed-112">Dette finnes i listen <STRONG>Faktureringsmetode</STRONG> i dialogboksen <STRONG>Opprett faktura</STRONG>.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="76bed-112">This is found in the <STRONG>Invoicing method</STRONG> list in the <STRONG>Create invoice</STRONG> dialog box.</span></span></P>

<span data-ttu-id="76bed-113">Hvis feltet **Tilbakefør avsetninger ved kreditering** i skjemaet **Servicestyringsparametere** er satt til **Manuell**, må du reversere transaksjonene for avsatt omsetning enkeltvis før du kan opprette et kreditnotaforslag for transaksjonen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="76bed-113">If the **Reverse accruals on crediting** field in the **Service management parameters** form is set to **Manual**, you have to reverse each accrued revenue transaction individually before you create a credit note proposal for the transaction.</span></span>
