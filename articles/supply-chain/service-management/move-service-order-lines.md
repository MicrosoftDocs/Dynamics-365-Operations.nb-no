[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]


1.  <span data-ttu-id="cfd40-104">Klikk på **Servicestyring** \> **Felles** \> **Serviceordrer** \> **Serviceordrer**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="cfd40-104">Click **Service management** \> **Common** \> **Service orders** \> **Service orders**.</span></span>

2.  <span data-ttu-id="cfd40-105">Dobbeltklikk serviceordren som inneholder linjene du vil flytte til en annen serviceordre.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="cfd40-105">Double-click the service order that contains the lines that you want to move to another service order.</span></span>
