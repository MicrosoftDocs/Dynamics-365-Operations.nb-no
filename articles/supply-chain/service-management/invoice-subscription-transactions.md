[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]



1.  <span data-ttu-id="0e2e1-104">Klikk på **Servicestyring** \> **Felles** \> **Serviceabonnementer** \> **Alle serviceabonnementer**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0e2e1-104">Click **Service management** \> **Common** \> **Service subscriptions** \> **All service subscriptions**.</span></span>
