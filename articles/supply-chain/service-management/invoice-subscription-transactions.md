# <a name="invoice-subscription-transactions"></a><span data-ttu-id="f6696-103">Fakturere abonnementtransaksjoner</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f6696-103">Invoice subscription transactions</span></span> 

[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]



1.  <span data-ttu-id="f6696-104">Klikk på **Servicestyring** \> **Felles** \> **Serviceabonnementer** \> **Alle serviceabonnementer**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f6696-104">Click **Service management** \> **Common** \> **Service subscriptions** \> **All service subscriptions**.</span></span>

3.  <span data-ttu-id="f6696-106">Klikk **Analyser** \> **Gebyrtransaksjoner**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f6696-106">Click **Analyze** \> **Fee transactions**.</span></span>

4.  <span data-ttu-id="f6696-107">Velg gebyrtransaksjonen(e) som skal faktureres, og klikk deretter **Funksjoner** \> **Fakturaforslag** for å åpne skjemaet **Fakturaforslag**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f6696-107">Select the fee transaction or fee transactions to invoice, and then click **Functions** \> **Invoice proposal** to open the **Invoice proposals** form.</span></span>

7.  <span data-ttu-id="f6696-110">Verifiser innholdet i fakturaen, og klikk **Poster faktura** for å åpne skjemaet **Poster faktura** for å postere fakturaen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f6696-110">Verify the contents of the invoice, click **Post invoice** to open the **Post invoice** form to post the invoice.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f6696-111">Du kan også skrive ut fakturaen fra skjemaet **Poster faktura**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f6696-111">You can also print the invoice from the **Post invoice** form.</span></span>
