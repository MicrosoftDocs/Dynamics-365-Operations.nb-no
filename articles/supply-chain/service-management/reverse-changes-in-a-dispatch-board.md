[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]


<span data-ttu-id="9a01f-104">Dette emnet beskriver hvordan du tilbakefører ulagrede endringer du gjør i en fordelingstavle.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9a01f-104">This topic describes how to reverse unsaved modifications that you make in a dispatch board.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="9a01f-105">Du tilordner for eksempel en arbeider til en tjenesteaktivitet, lagrer informasjonen, og bestemmer deg senere for å tilordne en annen arbeider til serviceaktiviteten.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9a01f-105">For example, you assign a worker to a service activity, save the information, and then later decide to assign a different worker to the service activity.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="9a01f-106">Du endrer arbeideren i tjenestefordelingen, og deretter, før du lagrer endringen, oppdager du at den tilordnede arbeideren ikke er tilgjengelig.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9a01f-106">You modify the worker in the dispatch board, and then, before saving the change, learn that the worker just assigned is not available.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="9a01f-107">Du kan tilbakestille den ulagrede endringen slik at den opprinnelige arbeideren blir tilordnet til serviceordren på nytt.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9a01f-107">You can reverse the unsaved modification so that the original worker is reassigned to the service order.</span></span>
