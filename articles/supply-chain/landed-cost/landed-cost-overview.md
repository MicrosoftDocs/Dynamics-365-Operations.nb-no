### <a name="item-ownership"></a><span data-ttu-id="79d10-120">Vareeierskap</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79d10-120">Item ownership</span></span>

### <a name="costs"></a><span data-ttu-id="79d10-126">Kostnader</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79d10-126">Costs</span></span>
