## <a name="purchase-order-statuses"></a><span data-ttu-id="8a494-145">Bestillingsstatuser</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8a494-145">Purchase order statuses</span></span>
<span data-ttu-id="8a494-146">Bestillinger inneholder flere statusfelt som viser fremdriften for bestillingen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8a494-146">POs include several status fields that indicate the progress of the order.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="8a494-147">Disse feltene er synlige i visningen **Topptekst** for ordren, og noen få av dem vises også i rutenettoversikt for alle ordrer.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8a494-147">All these fields are visible in the **Header** view of the order, and a few of them are also visible in the grid overview of all orders.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="8a494-148">Feltet **Status** viser statusen for et antall på bestillingen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8a494-148">The **Status** field show the status for quantities on the order.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="8a494-149">Følgende verdier er tilgjengelige:</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8a494-149">The following values are available:</span></span>
