3. <span data-ttu-id="1128d-108">Juster følgende innstillinger etter behov:</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="1128d-108">Adjust the following settings as appropriate:</span></span>

   - <span data-ttu-id="1128d-111">**Sluttdato**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="1128d-111">**End date**</span></span>
