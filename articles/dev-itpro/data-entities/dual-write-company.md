<span data-ttu-id="7226e-107">Common Data Service ikke har et tilsvarende begrep.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7226e-107">Common Data Service doesn't have an equivalent concept.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7226e-108">Det nærmeste konseptet er *forretningsenhet*, som hovedsakelig er en sikkerhets- og synlighetsgrense for brukerdata.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7226e-108">The closest concept is *business unit*, which is primarily a security and visibility boundary for user data.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7226e-109">Dette konseptet har ikke de samme juridiske eller forretningsmessige implikasjoner som selskapskonsept i Finance and Operations.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7226e-109">This concept doesn't have the same legal or business implications as the company concept in Finance and Operations.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="7226e-110">Fordi forretningsenhet og firma ikke er identiske konsepter, er det ikke mulig å tvinge en en-til-en (1:1) tilordning mellom dem for formålet Common Data Service-integrering.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7226e-110">Because business unit and company aren't equivalent concepts, it isn't possible to force a one-to-one (1:1) mapping between them for the purpose of Common Data Service integration.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7226e-111">Men fordi brukere må, som standard, kunne se de samme postene i Finance and Operations og Common Data Service, har Microsoft innført en ny enhet i Common Data Service som heter cdm\_firma.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7226e-111">However, because users must, by default, be able to see the same records in Finance and Operations and Common Data Service, Microsoft has introduced a new entity in Common Data Service that is named cdm\_Company.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7226e-112">Denne enheten tilsvarer firma-enheten i Finance and Operations.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7226e-112">This entity is equivalent to the Company entity in Finance and Operations.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7226e-113">For å garantere at synligheten av poster er ekvivalent mellom Finance and Operations og Common Data Service ut av boksen, anbefaler vi følgende oppsett for data i Common Data Service:</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7226e-113">To help guarantee that visibility of records is equivalent between Finance and Operations and Common Data Service out of the box, we recommend the following setup for data in Common Data Service:</span></span>
