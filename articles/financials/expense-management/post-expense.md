# <a name="post-an-expense-report"></a><span data-ttu-id="eb890-103">Poster en reiseregning</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eb890-103">Post an expense report</span></span>

[!INCLUDE [banner](../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="eb890-104">Når en reiseregning er godkjent og overført til økonomijournalen, kan den posteres i økonomimodulen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eb890-104">After an expense report has been approved and transferred to the general journal, it can be posted to the general ledger.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="eb890-105">Når du posterer en reiseregning, identifiseres utgifter som er kvalifisert for tilbakebetaling av merverdiavgiften (mva).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eb890-105">When you post an expense report, expenses that are eligible for recovery of value-added tax (VAT) are identified.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="eb890-106">Den ansatte som er ansvarlig for verifisering av reiseregningen tilordnes oppgaven med kontroll og tilbakebetaling av mva-betalinger.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eb890-106">The task of verifying and recovering VAT payments is assigned to the employee who is responsible for verifying the expense report.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="eb890-107">Hvis utgifter på en reiseregning skal belastes en annen leverandør enn firmaet den ansatte jobber for, må du kontrollere firmaet som skal betales for utgiftene, og firmaet som skal betale utgiftene.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eb890-107">If expenses on an expense report are charged to a company other than the company that employs the employee, you must verify the company that those expenses are owed to and the company that the expenses are owed from.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="eb890-108">Hvis, for eksempel, en ansatt som har sendt inn en reiseregning jobber for DAT-firmaet, men belaster DIR-firmaet for utgiftene.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eb890-108">For example, the employee who submitted an expense report works for the DAT company but charged an expense to the DIR company.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="eb890-109">I så fall skal DAT selskapet betales for utgiften, og DIR skal betale.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eb890-109">In this case, DAT is the company that the expense is owed to, and DIR is the company that the expense is owed from.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="eb890-110">Når du har kontrollert disse journallinjene, kan du postere utgiftslinjene til økonomimodulen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eb890-110">After you verify these journal lines, you can post the expense lines to the general ledger.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="eb890-111">Postere en reiseregning på siden **Godkjente reiseregninger**, velger du reiseregningen, og i handlingsruten velger du **Poster**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eb890-111">To post an expense report, on the **Approved expense reports** page, select the expense report, and then, on the Action Pane, select **Post**.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="eb890-112">Du kan også postere alle reiseregningene i listen samtidig.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eb890-112">You can also post all the expense reports in the list at the same time.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="eb890-113">Velg alle reiseregningene, og velg deretter **Poster**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eb890-113">Select all the expense reports, and then select **Post**.</span></span>
