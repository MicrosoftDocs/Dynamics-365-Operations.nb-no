1. <span data-ttu-id="020ea-106">Gå til Anleggsmidler > Anleggsmidler > Anleggsmidler.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="020ea-106">Go to Fixed assets > Fixed assets > Fixed assets.</span></span>
2. <span data-ttu-id="020ea-107">Klikk Ny.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="020ea-107">Click New.</span></span>
