<span data-ttu-id="adb30-108">Remburser brukes i internasjonale transaksjoner og bidrar til å garantere at betalinger blir foretatt.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="adb30-108">Letters of credit are used for international transactions and help guarantee that payments will be made.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="adb30-109">En remburs er en avtale som utstedes av en bank, der banken godtar å garantere betaling på vegne av en kjøper, så lenge vilkårene i avtalen mellom kjøper og selger er oppfylt.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="adb30-109">A letter of credit is an agreement that is issued by a bank, in which the bank agrees to guarantee payment on behalf of a buyer, provided that the terms of the agreement between the buyer and seller are met.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="adb30-110">En remburs også omtales som en vareremburs eller bankremburs.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="adb30-110">A letter of credit is also referred to as a documentary credit (DC).</span></span>

-   <span data-ttu-id="adb30-115">Mottakeren (selgeren) som skal motta betalingen</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="adb30-115">The beneficiary (seller) who will receive the payment</span></span>
-   <span data-ttu-id="adb30-116">Den utstedende banken som utsteder rembursen</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="adb30-116">The issuing bank that issues the letter of credit</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="adb30-120">En remburs kan enten være **gjenkallelig** eller **ugjenkallelig**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="adb30-120">A letter of credit can be **revocable** or **irrevocable**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="adb30-121">En remburs kan være **overførbar**, **ikke overførbar** eller **rullerende**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="adb30-121">The nature of a letter of credit can be **transferable**, **non-transferable**, or **revolving**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="adb30-122">En remburs er vanligvis en ugjenkallelig og bekreftet avtale om at betaling blir foretatt til en bestemt mottaker når komplett og nøyaktig forsendelsesdokumentasjon er sendt.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="adb30-122">Typically, a letter of credit is an irrevocable and confirmed agreement that payment will be made to a specific beneficiary upon submission of complete and accurate shipping documentation.</span></span>

## <a name="import-collections"></a><span data-ttu-id="adb30-123">Importinkassoer</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="adb30-123">Import collections</span></span>
<span data-ttu-id="adb30-124">En importinkasso er en avtale mellom banken og eksportøren (selgeren) der banken avtaler å levere forsendelsesdokumentasjonen til den internasjonale importøren (kjøperen).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="adb30-124">An import collection is an agreement between the bank and the exporter (seller), in which the bank agrees to deliver the shipping documentation to the international importer (buyer).</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="adb30-125">Banken forventes å levere forsendelsesdokumentasjonen ved mottak av kontant betaling for de sendte varene, eller ved mottak av en signert anvisning mot betalingen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="adb30-125">The bank is expected to deliver the shipping documentation upon receipt of payment for the shipped goods in cash, or upon receipt of a signed draft toward the payment.</span></span> 

<span data-ttu-id="adb30-126">En importinkasso er med på å garantere at selgeren betales når kjøperen henter forsendelsesdokumentene for å motta de importerte varene.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="adb30-126">An import collection helps guarantee that the seller is paid when the buyer collects the shipping documents to take delivery of the imported goods.</span></span>
