 - <span data-ttu-id="87a2c-111">Søkeren (kjøperen) som har til hensikt å betale for varene</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="87a2c-111">The applicant (buyer) who intends to pay for the goods</span></span> 
 - <span data-ttu-id="87a2c-112">Mottakeren (selgeren) som skal motta betalingen</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="87a2c-112">The beneficiary (seller) who will receive the payment</span></span>
 - <span data-ttu-id="87a2c-113">Den utstedende banken som utsteder rembursen</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="87a2c-113">The issuing bank that issues the letter of credit</span></span>
