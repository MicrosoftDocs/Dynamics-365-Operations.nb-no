## <a name="create-a-new-list"></a><span data-ttu-id="556a7-106">Opprette en ny liste</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="556a7-106">Create a new list</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="556a7-107">Hvis du vil opprette en kuratert produktanbefalingsliste, gjør du følgende.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="556a7-107">To create a curated product recommendation list, follow these steps.</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="556a7-108">Gå til **Detaljhandel og handel &gt; Produktanbefalinger &gt; Anbefalingslister**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="556a7-108">Go to **Retail and Commerce &gt; Product recommendations &gt; Recommendation lists**.</span></span>
1. <span data-ttu-id="556a7-109">Velg **Ny**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="556a7-109">Select **New**.</span></span>
1. <span data-ttu-id="556a7-110">Angi en verdi i **Liste-ID**-feltet.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="556a7-110">In the **List Id** field, enter a value.</span></span>
1. <span data-ttu-id="556a7-111">Angi en verdi i **Listenavn**-feltet.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="556a7-111">In the **List name** field, enter a value.</span></span>
    - <span data-ttu-id="556a7-112">**Listenavn** er tittelen på listen som vil vises i delen med kuraterte lister i modulen **Produktsamling** .</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="556a7-112">The **List name** is the title of the list that will appear in the curated lists section of the **Product collection** module.</span></span>
1. <span data-ttu-id="556a7-113">Hvis du vil legge til produkter i listen, velger du **Legg til produkter**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="556a7-113">To add products to the list, select **Add products**.</span></span>
1. <span data-ttu-id="556a7-114">Hvis du vil endre rekkefølgen på produktene i listen, angir du en verdi i **Vis rekkefølge**-kolonnen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="556a7-114">To change the order of the products in the list, enter a value in the **Display order** column.</span></span>
    - <span data-ttu-id="556a7-115">Hvis to produkter har samme verdi for visningsrekkefølge, kan den endelige rekkefølgen for disse to resultatene avvike fra back office.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="556a7-115">If two products have the same display order value, then the final order of those two results may differ from the back office.</span></span>
1. <span data-ttu-id="556a7-116">Velg **Lagre** for å lagre listen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="556a7-116">Select **Save** to save the list.</span></span>
