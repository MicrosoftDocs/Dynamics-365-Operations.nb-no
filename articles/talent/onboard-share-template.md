## <a name="share-an-onboarding-guide-with-other-contributors"></a><span data-ttu-id="389ed-105">Dele en veiledning for jobbintroduksjon med andre bidragsytere</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="389ed-105">Share an onboarding guide with other contributors</span></span>

    ![[<span data-ttu-id="389ed-108">Dele en veiledning for jobbintroduksjon med andre bidragsytere</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="389ed-108">Sharing an onboarding guide with other contributors</span></span>](./media/onboard-share-guide.png)](./media/onboard-share-guide.png)

    ![[<span data-ttu-id="389ed-111">Angi tilgangs- og e-postinnstillinger for en veiledning for jobbintroduksjon</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="389ed-111">Setting access and email preferences for an onboarding guide</span></span>](./media/onboard-share-guide-with-contributors.png)](./media/onboard-share-guide-with-contributors.png)

4. <span data-ttu-id="389ed-112">Skriv inn et navn eller en e-postadresse, og velg ønsket mottaker i rullegardinlisten.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="389ed-112">Enter a name or email address, and select the desired recipient in the drop-down list.</span></span>

6. <span data-ttu-id="389ed-115">Når du er ferdig med å legge til eller endre mottakere, velger du **Oppdater**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="389ed-115">When you've finished adding or modifying recipients, select **Update**.</span></span>

    <span data-ttu-id="389ed-119">[![Dele en mal med andre bidragsytere](./media/onboard-share-template.png)](./media/onboard-share-template.png)</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="389ed-119">[![Sharing a template with other contributors](./media/onboard-share-template.png)](./media/onboard-share-template.png)</span></span>

4. <span data-ttu-id="389ed-123">Skriv inn et navn eller en e-postadresse, og velg ønsket mottaker i rullegardinlisten.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="389ed-123">Enter a name or email address, and select the desired recipient in the drop-down list.</span></span>
