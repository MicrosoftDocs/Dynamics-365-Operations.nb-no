# <a name="prevent-discounts-for-retail-products"></a><span data-ttu-id="6628c-103">Hindre rabatter for detaljhandelsprodukter</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6628c-103">Prevent discounts for retail products</span></span>

[!include[banner](includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="6628c-104">Det finnes ulike årsaker til hvorfor forhandlere vil forhindre at noen produkter rabatteres, fra en kampanje eller ved salget på salgsstedet.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6628c-104">There are various reasons why retailers may want to prevent some products from being discounted, either from a promotion or during the sale at the POS.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="6628c-105">De følgende alternativene, som finnes på **Detaljhandel**-kategorien for frigitte produkter, gjør at produktet kan konfigureres for å hindre alle eller manuelle rabatter.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6628c-105">The following options, which can be found on the **Retail** tab of released products, will allow the product to be configured to prevent all or manual discounts.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6628c-106">Innstillingene kan også angis på kategorinivå fra kategorihierarkiet for detaljhandel.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6628c-106">The settings can also be specified at the category level from the retail category hierarchy.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="6628c-107">**Forhindre alle rabatter**: Velg dette alternativet for å forhindre at alle typer rabatter brukes på dette produktet.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6628c-107">**Prevent all discounts**: Select this option to prevent all types of discounts from being applied to this product.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6628c-108">Dette inkluderer kampanjer som samlerabatt, antalls- og terskelrabatter, i tillegg til rabatter for manuelle linjerabatter og transaksjonsrabatter som brukes under et salg av en POS-bruker.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6628c-108">This includes promotions such as mix and match, quantity and threshold discounts, as well as manual line and transaction discounts that are applied during a sale by a POS user.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="6628c-109">**Hindre manuelle rabatter**: Velg dette alternativet for bare å hindre de manuelle linjerabattene eller transaksjonsrabattene som brukes under et salg av en POS-bruker.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6628c-109">**Prevent manual discounts**: Select this option to only prevent the manual line or transaction discounts that are applied during a sale by a POS user.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6628c-110">Produkter med dette alternativet valgt er fremdeles kvalifisert for kampanjer, for eksempel samlerabatt, antalls- og terskelrabatter.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6628c-110">Products with this option selected are still eligible for promotions, such as mix and match and quantity and threshold discounts.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="6628c-111">**Merk**: Disse innstillingene begrenser ikke operasjonen for prisoverstyring, fordi det angir basisprisen og behandles ikke som en rabatt.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6628c-111">**Note**: These settings do not restrict the price override operation, because that sets the base price and is not treated as a discount.</span></span>
