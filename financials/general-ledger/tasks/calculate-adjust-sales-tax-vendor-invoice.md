# <a name="calculate-and-adjust-sales-tax-on-a-vendor-invoice"></a><span data-ttu-id="af01c-103">Beregne og justere merverdiavgift på en leverandørfaktura</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="af01c-103">Calculate and adjust sales tax on a vendor invoice</span></span>

[!include[task guide banner](../../includes/task-guide-banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="af01c-104">Hvis det opprinnelige kildedokumentet viser forskjellige mva-beløp som er beregnet, kan du justere disse beløpene før postering.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="af01c-104">If the original source document displays different tax amounts as calculated, you can adjust those amounts before posting.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="af01c-105">Denne oppgaven bruker demonstrasjonsfirmaet DEMF.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="af01c-105">This task uses the DEMF demo company.</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="af01c-106">Gå til Leverandører > Fakturaer > Fakturajournal.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="af01c-106">Go to Accounts payable > Invoices > Invoice journal.</span></span>
2. <span data-ttu-id="af01c-107">Klikk Ny.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="af01c-107">Click New.</span></span>
3. <span data-ttu-id="af01c-108">Merk den valgte raden i listen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="af01c-108">In the list, mark the selected row.</span></span>
4. <span data-ttu-id="af01c-109">Klikk rullegardinknappen i Navn-feltet for å åpne oppslaget.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="af01c-109">In the Name field, click the drop-down button to open the lookup.</span></span>
5. <span data-ttu-id="af01c-110">Klikk koblingen i den valgte raden i listen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="af01c-110">In the list, click the link in the selected row.</span></span>
6. <span data-ttu-id="af01c-111">Klikk Linjer.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="af01c-111">Click Lines.</span></span>
7. <span data-ttu-id="af01c-112">Merk den valgte raden i listen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="af01c-112">In the list, mark the selected row.</span></span>
8. <span data-ttu-id="af01c-113">Angi de ønskede verdiene i Konto-feltet.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="af01c-113">In the Account field, specify the desired values.</span></span>
9. <span data-ttu-id="af01c-114">Skriv inn en verdi i Faktura-feltet.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="af01c-114">In the Invoice field, type a value.</span></span>
10. <span data-ttu-id="af01c-115">Angi et tall i Kredit-feltet.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="af01c-115">In the Credit field, enter a number.</span></span>
11. <span data-ttu-id="af01c-116">Angi ønskede verdier i Motkonto-feltet.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="af01c-116">In the Offset account field, specify the desired values.</span></span>
12. <span data-ttu-id="af01c-117">Klikk Merverdiavgift.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="af01c-117">Click Sales tax.</span></span>
13. <span data-ttu-id="af01c-118">Angi et tall i feltet Totalt faktisk mva-beløp.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="af01c-118">In the Total actual sales tax amount field, enter a number.</span></span>
14. <span data-ttu-id="af01c-119">Klikk OK.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="af01c-119">Click OK.</span></span>
15. <span data-ttu-id="af01c-120">Klikk Lagre.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="af01c-120">Click Save.</span></span>
16. <span data-ttu-id="af01c-121">Klikk Merverdiavgift.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="af01c-121">Click Sales tax.</span></span>
17. <span data-ttu-id="af01c-122">Mva-beløp kan justeres per mva-kode i kategorien Justering.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="af01c-122">On the Adjustment tab, the sales tax amounts can be adjusted per sales tax code.</span></span>
18. <span data-ttu-id="af01c-123">Klikk Tilbakestill faktiske fra beregnede beløp.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="af01c-123">Click Reset actual from calculated amounts.</span></span>
19. <span data-ttu-id="af01c-124">Klikk OK.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="af01c-124">Click OK.</span></span>
20. <span data-ttu-id="af01c-125">Klikk Lagre.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="af01c-125">Click Save.</span></span>
